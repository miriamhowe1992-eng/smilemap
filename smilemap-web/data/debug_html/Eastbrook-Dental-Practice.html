Urgent or emergency dental care

If you have a sudden dental problem, like pain, bleeding or a cracked tooth, contact this dentist to book an appointment for urgent or emergency dental care. They may be able to offer you:

  • an urgent appointment at short notice
  • advice where to get out-of-hours treatment

If you still need help, check your dental symptoms with NHS 111 online or call 111.
